The Pirates of Penzance

Act I – A rocky seashore on the coast of Cornwall

[A rocky seashore. On one side a cavern, on the other a rustic bridge.]

FRED

Oh, is there not one maiden breast
Which would be freed from cold distress?

RUTH

Oh sir, despair not, but take heart!

FRED

Alas! I fear I am alone.

PIRATE KING

For I am a Pirate King!

CHORUS

Hail! Hail! Hail!

(The pirates gather triumphantly around their leader.)

SAMUEL

Then we’ll give three cheers and one cheer more!
